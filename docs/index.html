Protecting what's important

Absolute Security & Electrical is a family-run business with over 20 years of experience in the electrical and security industry in Northern Ireland. We provide bespoke, cost-effective solutions to all your security requirements, backed by the highest level of customer care.

What We Do

Automated Gates

Complete automation for existing and new gates. Both wooden and wrought iron gates can be supplied and automated. We offer a complete package, including ground preparation, pillar construction, and outdoor electrical supply.

  • Underground kits & worm drive automation
  • Articulated arm & external sliding kits
  • Video, audio, or keypad entry systems
  • Induction loops (auto opening)

CCTV

Monitor your home or business at the touch of a button. We offer solutions from single to multiple cameras that can be viewed offsite via the internet on your smartphone, laptop, or PC.

  • Digital Video Recorders (DVRs)
  • Continuous, scheduled, or motion recording
  • IR Bullet & PTZ Dome cameras
  • Wireless camera options

Intruder Alarms

Protect your family and home from intruders with hard-wired alarms. We use only the best products available and endeavour to conceal all cables, discussing possible routes prior to installation.

  • Systems tailored to your budget
  • Instant contact upon activation
  • Complete perimeter and internal security

Security Lighting

Security lighting is an essential element of your home security. Any type or style of outdoor light can be installed.

  • Motion sensor activation
  • Timer and photocell operation
  • Cost-effective deterrent

Access Control

Control and monitor who enters your building. Ideal for multiple occupancy buildings or restricting access to specific staff members.

  • Video door entry
  • Swipe card & contactless smart cards
  • Key fobs
  • Clocking on / attendance recording

IT Networks

We install IT networks for homes, offices, and businesses. Hard-wired cabling is installed throughout the building, returning to a central hub to distribute voice and data applications.

  • CAT 5 / CAT 6 cabling
  • Fibre optic networks for larger bespoke systems
