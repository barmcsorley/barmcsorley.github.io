What We Do

Automated Gates

Complete automation for new and existing wooden or wrought iron gates. We provide an end-to-end service, from groundworks and pillar construction to electrical supply and seamless system integration.

  • Underground & worm drive automation
  • Articulated arm & sliding gate kits
  • Smart video, audio & keypad intercoms
  • Auto-opening induction loops

CCTV Systems

Monitor your home or business in high definition from anywhere in the world. We install scalable camera systems that deliver crystal-clear footage directly to your smartphone or tablet.

  • High-definition IP and 4K cameras
  • Smart motion detection & instant mobile alerts
  • Colour night vision & PTZ domes
  • Secure local NVR & cloud storage options

Intruder Alarms

Protect your property with advanced, highly reliable alarm systems. We expertly conceal wiring and design perimeter defenses that provide total peace of mind, whether you're at home or away.

  • Smart alarms with instant app notifications
  • Tailored wired and hybrid wireless systems
  • Complete internal & perimeter protection
  • Remote arming and system monitoring

Security Lighting

A powerful first line of defense. We design and install modern, energy-efficient outdoor lighting systems that deter intruders and safely illuminate your property.

  • Ultra-bright, energy-efficient LED floodlights
  • Smart motion sensor activation
  • Dusk-to-dawn photocell operation
  • Architectural & perimeter security lighting

Access Control

Secure your premises by controlling exactly who enters your building. Ideal for commercial spaces, multi-occupancy buildings, and restricting access to staff-only areas.

  • Video door entry & smart intercoms
  • Keyless entry: fobs, swipe cards & mobile credentials
  • Biometric access solutions
  • Time and attendance tracking

IT Networks & Wi-Fi

Robust connectivity for modern homes and businesses. We install structured data cabling to centralise your network, ensuring fast, drop-free internet in every room.

  • High-speed CAT6 and CAT6a data cabling
  • Wi-Fi access points & seamless mesh networks
  • Fibre optic links for large sites & outbuildings
  • Smart home & office network integration
